The spatial structure of contemporary cities has been transformed according to the logic of capital. Seoul is a city in which this situation is dramatic. The city attributable to capitalism covered the entire land with a uniform residential type, and the high wall and the apartments towering beyond it became the monotonous landscape that can be easily encountered in Seoul.
          However, this standardization acts as a control variable capable of capturing novelty and operates the whole of Seoul as a laboratory. This is because it is easy to capture the fragmented desires and lives of urban people in a homogeneous urban dwelling.
          In <Apatopia>, based on the manipulated variables captured in a laboratory called Seoul, it attempts to discover the beginning of the future city and interpret it dramatically. As a result, <Clues of Apatopia> arbitrarily interprets the future of the city based on reality.
